Our Story

Founded over 25 years ago, Owens and Associates began with a simple yet powerful mission: to help families stay together during challenging times. What started as a small local service has grown into a trusted partner for families across the region.

Throughout our journey, we've remained steadfast in our commitment to quality service and family support. Our team of dedicated professionals brings decades of combined experience in family services, child welfare, and community support.

1999

Foundation

Owens and Associates was established with a mission to support families in crisis.

2005

Expansion

Added comprehensive drug testing and supervised visitation services.

2015

Innovation

Launched skills development programs and in-home safety assessments.

Growth

Celebrating 25+ years of service and helping over 1000 families.
